My Story

My journey began in the fast-paced world of tech, where I spent 8 years as a product manager at Google. On the surface, I had achieved the "dream career" - great salary, impressive title, and exciting projects. But beneath that shiny exterior, I was struggling with burnout, anxiety, and a growing sense that something was missing.

After hitting my lowest point in 2018, I took a six-month sabbatical that completely transformed my perspective. I traveled, volunteered, and most importantly, gave myself permission to rest and reflect. This break led me to discover my passion for mental health advocacy and helping others navigate similar challenges.

Today, I combine my product design expertise with mental health coaching to help creative professionals build sustainable careers. My approach is rooted in the belief that true success comes from aligning your work with your values, setting healthy boundaries, and embracing imperfection.

Through my 1:1 sessions and workshops, I've helped over 200 professionals redesign their careers with intention and self-compassion. My story isn't about having all the answers - it's about learning to ask better questions and find your own path forward.
