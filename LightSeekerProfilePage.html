About Me

Hello! I'm Alex, a marketing professional making the exciting but daunting transition into UX design. After 5 years in digital marketing, I realized my true passion lies in creating user experiences that are not just functional but delightful.


I've been taking online courses in UX/UI design and working on personal projects to build my portfolio. While I love the creative aspects, I sometimes struggle with imposter syndrome and the technical side of design tools.


I'm looking to connect with Lights who've made similar career transitions or who can share insights about breaking into the design industry. I'm particularly interested in learning about:

  • Building a portfolio without professional experience
  • Effective ways to learn design tools
  • Navigating the job search process
  • Balancing learning with current job responsibilities
